SMOS Speaker discrimination experiment

Determine whether two voices are from the same person

Thank you for participating in this experiment!

In this experiment, we use computer zero-shot synthesis and real of human speech. Each question has two audio files. After listening with headphones, answer the questions based on the speaker similarity of the two audio files (only the timbre of the speaker is considered, not the content of the sentence).

The scoring criteria are:

  • Sure to be the same speaker (absolutely sure)
  • Not sure, but tend to think that they are the same speaker (same, not sure)
  • Not tend to think they are same speaker or not
  • Not sure, but tend to think that they are different speakers (different, not sure)
  • Sure to be a different speaker (different, absolutely sure)
